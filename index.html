Overview

BrainSIS is a highly specialized engineering team dedicated to providing comprehensive Design & Engineering /SITC scope in the field of Process Automation. Our range of services includes engineering of Control system, Safety Instrumented System(SIS), and Documentation. We offer end-to-end scope from concept to handover.

Core Expertise

Within the realm of functional safety, we possess a robust background in engineering solutions for:

  • Emergency Shutdown System (ESD)
  • Instrumented Protective System (IPS)
  • Burner Management System (BMS)
  • Fire and Gas System (FGS)
  • Fire Alarm System (FAS)
  • PAGA and High Integrity Pressure Protection System (HIPPS)

Our solutions comply with international standards such as IEC61508/61850 and NFPA.
